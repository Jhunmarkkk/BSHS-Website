About Us

Welcome to MSHS

Manila Science High School, the Philippines' first science high school, is dedicated to developing students' potential in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. With a curriculum that balances advanced sciences and mathematics with humanities and research, it prepares students for excellence in academics and life. The school nurtures innovation, critical thinking, and creativity, empowering learners to solve real-world problems. Located in Manila, it remains a leading institution for aspiring young scientists and leaders.
